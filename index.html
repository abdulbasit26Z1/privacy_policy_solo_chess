Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2023

Welcome to Solo Chess by Az Meer. Your privacy is critically important to us. This policy explains how we handle data within the application.

1. Data Collection

Solo Chess by Az Meer is designed as an offline-first strategy game. We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information such as your name, address, or phone number to play the game.

2. Third-Party Services

To provide a professional experience and support development, our app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. These include:

  • Google Play Services: Used for app stability and performance tracking.
  • AdMob: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use cookies or unique device identifiers to show personalized ads based on your interests.

3. Audio and Media

The app accesses your device's local resources to play background music and sound effects. This data stays entirely on your device and is never uploaded to any external servers.

4. Children’s Privacy

Solo Chess by Az Meer is a family-friendly strategy game. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent and aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can take necessary actions.

5. Security

We value your trust in providing us your information. However, remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Any changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
